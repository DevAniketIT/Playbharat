Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Creating a channel is easy! After signing up, go to your profile dashboard and click "Create Channel". Follow our step-by-step wizard to customize your channel with a name, description, banner, and regional preferences. You'll be ready to upload your first video in minutes!

PlayBharat supports MP4, AVI, MOV, and WebM formats. We recommend MP4 with H.264 codec for optimal quality and compatibility. Maximum file size is 2GB, with support for resolutions up to 4K. Our system automatically optimizes videos for different devices and connection speeds.

Join our Creator Partner Program once you reach 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in 12 months. Monetization options include video ads, channel memberships, Super Chat, brand partnerships, and merchandise integration. We support Indian payment methods including UPI, bank transfers, and digital wallets.

PlayBharat supports 15+ Indian languages including Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, and more. You can set your preferred language, add subtitles in multiple languages, and use our auto-translation features for wider reach.

Use the "Report" button on any video, comment, or channel. Select the appropriate reason (spam, harassment, copyright, etc.) and provide details. Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety concerns, email report@playbharat.in directly.
