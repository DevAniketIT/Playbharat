Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Enter your email, username, and password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile with your location and language preferences

Once registered, you can start watching, liking, and commenting on videos immediately!

To upload videos:

  1. Create a channel first from your profile settings
  2. Click "Upload" in the top navigation
  3. Select your video file (MP4, MOV, AVI formats supported)
  4. Add title, description, and select category
  5. Choose thumbnail and privacy settings
  6. Click "Publish" to make your video live

Video Requirements: Max 500MB, up to 10 minutes duration for new creators.

Creating a channel allows you to upload content:

  1. Go to your profile page
  2. Click "Create Channel" button
  3. Enter channel name and description
  4. Choose your content category and target audience
  5. Upload channel banner and profile picture
  6. Review and create your channel

Your channel will be reviewed within 24 hours and activated once approved.

Allowed Content:

  • Educational and informational videos
  • Entertainment content (comedy, music, dance)
  • Cultural and traditional content
  • News and current affairs
  • Sports and gaming content
  • Cooking and lifestyle videos

Prohibited Content: Hate speech, violence, adult content, copyright violations, spam, and harmful content.

Review our Community Guidelines for detailed policies.

Common solutions:

  1. Check your internet connection - Videos require stable internet
  2. Clear browser cache - Old cached data can cause issues
  3. Try a different browser - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge supported
  4. Disable ad blockers - Some extensions interfere with video playback
  5. Update your browser - Use the latest version for best performance

If issues persist, contact our support team with details about your device and browser.
