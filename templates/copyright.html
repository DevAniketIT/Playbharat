Protecting creators and respecting intellectual property rights

Our Commitment to Copyright Protection

PlayBharat respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. We are committed to protecting creators' rights while fostering a creative and diverse platform for Indian content.

Copyright Infringement Policy

  • We respond promptly to valid copyright infringement claims
  • Infringing content is removed as per DMCA guidelines
  • Repeat offenders face account suspension or termination
  • We provide counter-notification procedures for disputed claims

Filing a Copyright Complaint

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission, please provide:

Required Information
  • Description of copyrighted work
  • Location of infringing content
  • Your contact information
  • Good faith statement
  • Sworn statement of accuracy
